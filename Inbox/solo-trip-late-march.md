# Solo Trip — Late March / Early April

**Goal:** 2 nights, solo, active, decompression from family/work burnout.
**Timing:** Late March – early April 2026

## Top Options

### Snowboarding
- **Mammoth Mountain, CA** — Often open into May. Great late-season conditions and solo-friendly mountain.
- **Snowbird, UT** — Open late (sometimes May), 30 min from SLC. World-class terrain.
- **Breckenridge / Telluride, CO** — Both typically open through early April. Telluride is quieter and stunning.
- **Mt. Bachelor, Bend OR** — Late-season snow + Bend is a great solo town (food, beer, low-key vibe).

### Non-Snow Active (backup / alternative)
- **Moab, UT** — Mountain biking + hiking. Perfect spring temps, physically demanding in the best way.
- **Sedona, AZ** — Red rock hiking/biking. Warm, beautiful, genuinely restorative.

## Top Pick
**Mt. Bachelor / Bend, OR** — Best of both worlds. Ski if snow is good, hike if not. Easy solo trip, not overwhelming.
