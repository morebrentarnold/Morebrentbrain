# Questions to Ask Employers

## About the Role
- What does success look like in this role in the first 30/60/90 days?
- What are the biggest challenges facing someone in this position?
- How has this role evolved over time?
- Why is this position open?

## About the Team
- Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with?
- What's the team dynamic like?
- How does the team collaborate and communicate?
- What are the team's current priorities?

## About Company Culture
- How would you describe the company culture?
- What do you enjoy most about working here?
- How does the company support professional development?
- How does the company handle work-life balance?

## About Growth & Impact
- What are the opportunities for growth and advancement?
- How does the company measure success/impact?
- What are the company's goals for the next year?
- How does this role contribute to those goals?

## About Process & Logistics
- What are the next steps in the interview process?
- What's the timeline for making a decision?
- Is there anything else I can provide to help in the evaluation?

## Red Flag Questions (Ask Tactfully)
- What's the turnover rate on this team?
- Why did the last person leave this role?
- What's the biggest challenge the team/company is facing right now?
