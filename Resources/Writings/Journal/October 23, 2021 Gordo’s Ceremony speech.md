# October 23, 2021: Gordo’s Ceremony speech

* Hey everyone
* Because were all a little sick in this family
  * Ive thought about this day for a long time
* Honest I’m a bit speechless 
* But one thing 
  * how lucky I’ve been to have had my parents and Gord and Doro raise me.
* But what I realized is that 
  * we’ve all been lucky to have them in our lives

* So because Im speechless 
  * and instead of picking on the old man
  * I thought he could capture this moment better than me
  * I would read some of his letters  from over the past 20 years

* I’ve tried to keep the original writing in tact as much as possible, and I could understand it!
  * Had to write everything down because I was afraid I couldn’t actually read his handwriting. 
  * So apologies for looking at my phone.

* He would write letters over few days

>
