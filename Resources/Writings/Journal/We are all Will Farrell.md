
**Cutural icon**
A "cultural icon" is a person, symbol, object, or entity widely recognized as representing significant cultural values, beliefs, or identity within a particular society; essentially, something that serves as a recognizable symbol of a culture and its people, often embodying important ideas or ideals. 

Examples of cultural icons:
* Individuals: Martin Luther King Jr. (civil rights), Nelson Mandela (anti-apartheid), Marilyn Monroe (Hollywood glamour)  
* Landmarks: The Statue of Liberty (American democracy), Eiffel Tower (French culture), Great Wall of China (Chinese history)  
* Fictional characters: Mickey Mouse (Disney, childhood), Batman (superheroism)
