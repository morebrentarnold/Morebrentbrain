Working backwards in my day, I realized  I didn’t want to die anytime soon. I know that sounds cliche or corny. But I’ve never contemplated the real absence of me. 

I thought of myself having a heart attack. And it felt closer than I’ve ever thought. My diet has to change now. I need to be here for a longer time for Billie and Jane. I need to make sure they are safe and secure. I want to take in all of their being, in one long breath.

I felt a closeness between youth and age that I had never connected. 40 felt at once old and young at the same time.
