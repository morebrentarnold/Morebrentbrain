## How to Submit Claims

### Dr. Jade (Jane's Psychiatrist) — Out of Network
Check with your insurance for out-of-network reimbursement using the following CPT codes:

| Appointment | CPT Code(s) | Rate |
|---|---|---|
| First appointment | 90792 | $650 |
| Follow-up | 99213 + 90833 | $200 + $200 = $400 |

**Virtual sessions:** append telehealth specifier **95** to each code (e.g. 99213-95, 90833-95)
